About Me

Hi, I’m Zulqarnain Abu Hassan — a journalist, lecturer, and Silat Gayong instructor.

My work revolves around people, culture, and storytelling — from classroom insights and community engagement to the discipline of martial arts.

I am currently an entertainment journalist and academic in mass communication, passionate about developing young storytellers who think critically and write with heart.

Beyond the newsroom and classroom, I serve as a Silat Gayong instructor, mentoring young pesilat to uphold discipline, heritage, and respect through traditional martial arts.

My approach combines modern media practice and cultural grounding, reflecting a belief that education and storytelling can empower communities.
